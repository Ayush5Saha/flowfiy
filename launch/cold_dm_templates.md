## RULES FOR SENDING DMs

1. **Always reference something specific** — their company, their post, their role. Never copy-paste identical messages.
2. **Lead with their situation**, not your product
3. **One soft CTA** — never ask for a call in the first message
4. **30-50 per day max** on LinkedIn (above this risks restriction)
5. **Follow up once** if no reply after 3 days — one line: "Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried — happy to answer any questions."
6. **Track replies** in a simple spreadsheet: Name | Company | Platform | Date | Status
