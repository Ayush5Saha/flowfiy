## Post 2 — The Insight Post (Day 3)

Most cold email advice is about the email.

The real problem is upstream.

You can have a perfect subject line and still get ignored because:
- You emailed the wrong company
- You emailed at the wrong time (no growth signal, no pain)
- You wrote to a company that already has a solution

The research layer is what determines campaign quality. The email is just the expression of it.

This is why I built the Company Analyzer step into Flowfiy.

Before writing a single word, the AI:
- Reads the company's website
- Identifies their acquisition gaps
- Assesses their marketing maturity
- Finds recent signals (new hires, new service pages, funding language)
- Scores the fit against your ICP

Only then does it write the email — with a hook grounded in something real.

The difference in reply rates between "generic personalized" and "genuinely researched" is not small.

If you want to try it on your ICP — flowfiy.com (free tier available)
