# Outreach Templates — Flowfiy Outbound Operator

Every message must be personalized using real information found in the gap analysis. The goal is to start a conversation, not to close a deal in message #1.

---

## Core Rules for All Messages

1. **Reference something real** — always name a specific thing you observed about their business
2. **Name the gap** — connect that observation to the outbound automation problem Flowfiy solves
3. **Keep it short** — email under 120 words, WhatsApp under 80 words
4. **One CTA only** — "Worth a quick chat?" or "Book a free demo at flowfiy.co"
5. **No buzzwords** — avoid "revolutionary", "cutting-edge", "leverage", "AI-powered" (use "AI" sparingly)
6. **Founder tone** — write like a smart founder reaching out to another founder, not a salesperson hitting quota
7. **No pressure** — soft ask, easy to say yes or ignore

## Reply Handling Cheat Sheet

| Reply Type | How to Respond |
|---|---|
| "How much does it cost?" | "Depends on the use case — rather than just sending a price list, easier to show you what the pipeline does in 10 minutes and see if it makes sense. Are you free [time]?" |
| "Not interested right now" | "Totally fair. Any specific reason — is it timing or just not a priority? (No pressure, genuinely curious.)" |
| "We already have Apollo" | "Got it — do you have the full stack (Clay, Instantly, etc.) or just Apollo? Flowfiy replaces the combination, not just Apollo alone." |
| "Send me more info" | Send a short explanation of the 5-agent pipeline + pricing tiers + link to flowfiy.co |
| "Sounds interesting" | "Great — 15 minutes this week? I'll walk you through a live run of the pipeline." |
| "Unsubscribe / stop emailing" | "Of course — apologies for the interruption. No further emails from me." [Mark CRM as Do Not Contact] |
